# Code of Conduct

Fleet is an operator tool for local agent fleets. The project welcomes practical, respectful collaboration from people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming concrete problems.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce issues or understand proposals.
- Respect privacy. Do not post secrets, tokens, private logs, customer data, or other people's personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for a course correction.
- Security disclosures that include exploitable details in public issues before maintainers have had a chance to respond privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

For security issues, use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For general conduct concerns, open a minimal issue asking for maintainer contact without including private details.
